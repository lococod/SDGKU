How to Make Coffee

How to Make Coffee By CLMoreland in CookingCoffee14,5511 licenseDownloadFavorite
This how to is about making coffee. Making coffee for people who have never made it before could be difficult, this is why you need the how to. I will teach you step by step how to make the best coffee!

Add Teacher Note Step 1: Ingredients Picture of Ingredients Make sure have all of the provided ingredients below. Coffee grounds Coffee filters Sugar Creamer Coffee pot Cleaning supplies
Step 2: Placing Filter Picture of Placing Filter Place filter in the black filter holder displayed.
Step 3: Coffee Grounds Picture of Coffee Grounds Picture of Coffee Grounds Place the coffee grounds in the filter. You will need to place 4 tablespoons of coffee grounds for 8 cups. Make sure the coffee grounds are evenly spread in the filter.
Step 4: Water Picture of Water Add water to your coffee pot. Fill the water to line 8 for 8 cups.
Step 5: Adding Water to the Coffee Maker Picture of Adding Water to the Coffee Maker pour the water into the coffee pot carefully. Make sure to get all the water into the coffee maker.
Step 6: Placing Coffee Pot Picture of Placing Coffee Pot Place coffee pot back on burner with handle turned.
Step 7: Checking Your Coffee Pots Settings Picture of Checking Your Coffee Pots Settings Turn the coffee pot on to check the settings Tap the select/off button to see what settings are on Choose whether you want your coffee strength strong or regular Make sure you tap the select/off button once to brew now; twice to delay brew. We will brew now.
Step 8: Turning the Coffee Pot On Picture of Turning the Coffee Pot On Tap the select/off button once to turn on your coffee pot. Make sure the right strength is on Make sure the brew now light is on Make sure the glass light is on
Step 9: Cleaning Your Area Picture of Cleaning Your Area Properly clean up any spills that were made from the water or coffee grounds. Step 10: Brewing Picture of Brewing Your coffee will brew Once your coffee is done brewing you can finish up by fixing a cup of coffee and enjoying!
